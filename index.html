Publicis Sapient

Starting at Publicis Sapient in 2018, I joined the RBS account. As a team of four developers, we built a POC, in a series of 6 2-week sprints, for a new ‘Credit Wallet’ app for NatWest. We built a PWA using React.js, Redux and Styled Components. We used storybook to create a library of components which the app could then consume and use where needed.

During the migration, we have also taken the chance to enhance the APIs. I have led the development of a series of pages and features and am the longest standing developer on the team. I have also presented our achievements to the rest of the technology department, about 100 people.

The Spice Drawer

Personal

The Spice Drawer

This is a website I have been working on in my spare time just for myself, to store all my go-to recipes for easy accessibility. It is a work in progress to which I like to build on when I have the time; adding recipes and building to the functionality. My idea was to start lean and form a very simple website where I can just view my favourite recipes, with simplified methods and a few personal tweaks. To build the front-end I am using Vue.js and Vuex. For the back-end I am using Node.js which hooks up to a Firebase Realtime Database. This allows me to add recipes on the go from anywhere. Finally, I am using Netlify for the deployment.

www.thespicedrawer.co.uk
